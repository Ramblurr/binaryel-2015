Why Roll 20?

Why not the other virtual tabletops?

Roll 20 is Everywhere

More people can come together to play tabletop games than ever before, thanks to Roll 20's cutting edge technology. Players and GMs can game together with anyone from across the world, on PCs, Macs, smartphones, and tablets.

Roll 20 is Sustainable

After a massively successful Kickstarter in 2012, Roll 2012 is still growing and expanding with support from its user base: the players and GMs who game online.

Roll 20 is Ready For Your Game

Unlike many other virtual tabletops, Roll 20 really is system agnostic. It's features are suitable for all game types from rules light theater-of-the mind narrative play to the crunchiest wargame simulators.
